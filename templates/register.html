What is PARANOIA Chat and How does it work?

PARANOIA Chat is an encrypted chat service that does NOT know how to decrypt your chat. The server uses a separate encryption algorithm to encrypt your login independent of your saved messages which are encrypted in your side.

The server never sees your encryption key and does not know how to decrypt your messages.

Your chat partner must know the encryption key and put it into his paranoia client to read your messages.
